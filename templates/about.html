About Me

Hi there, I'm Claudia Guarente. Founder and Practitioner of Startup Interventions Ltd. Thank you for taking the time to visit our site and learn more about how we can help. Please get in touch via our Contact Page.

About Me:

With over 25 years' experience in dealing with children who experience difficulties in the classroom, I have worked as a Teaching Assistant, Learning Mentor, Lead Professional for Student Inclusion and Safeguarding Officer. I have studied or worked with professionals from many areas of children's services, which include:

  • Child Bereavement training from the Child Bereavement Trust, Bucks
  • Behaviour Management
  • Anger Management – trained by Primary PRU
  • Training From CAMHS and Educational Psychologist in Child Mental Health
  • Active Listening, Small Group work
  • Nurture Group Trained by Educational Psychologist and CAMHS team

How we Help – the Specifics:

With personalised SMART TARGETS

  • Specific: “To deliver a programme of. (ie Anger Management) for .......weeks that reduces the number of situations where anger/bullying/other ............. is observed during a .....week period.
  • Measureable: the evaluation and feedback (ie SDQ’s other analysis – quantative and qualitative), get feedback from the students, not using their real names.
  • Achievable: it will be agreed that the logistics of the Course or workshop is achieved in the time allocated. It may be that further work will be crucial to the required outcomes (to be discussed).
  • Relative: the workshops or course should be relative to the needs of students and easily accessed by the students in a way that can be evaluated by them.
  • Timely: the course should be conducted in a timely fashion that students can enjoy and take something from.
